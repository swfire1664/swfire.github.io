Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.

We collect information about you for 2 reasons: firstly, to provide you with pricing for our services and secondly, to provide you with our service.

We will only share your data if you give us explicit consent; we will always ask before this happens.

We will not send marketing emails to you unless we have your consent. We will give you the chance to refuse any marketing emails from us in the future.

The type of information we will collect about you is limited to the following:

  • Your Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Company Name
  • Company Address

We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.

The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies, we will delete or correct it promptly.

What we may contact you about:

  • Scheduling work
  • Courtesy calls regarding work previously carried out
  • Gathering any information vital to the completion of our services

The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our data policy and the law.

If you have any questions/comments about privacy, you should email us.
